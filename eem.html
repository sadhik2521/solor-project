Buying A Solar Power System on EMIs: Is It a Viable Choice?

Although it is quite easy to get a loan for solar power plants today, it is not uncommon to evaluate if doing so is viable. However, there are several benefits of opting for solar loans, such as:

  • # Since buying a solar panel system is a considerable investment, opting for EMIs increases affordability as you can choose a payment plan that will not burn a hole through your pocket.
  • # Solar finance options are available from reliable, trusted, and leading banks and NBFCs.
  • # In addition to several payment plans, a loan for solar systems comes at attractive rates, offers, and terms, thereby increasing flexibility.
  • # You can also go for no-cost EMIs, meaning you won't have to pay any interest on the monthly instalments, which is an excellent added advantage.
  • # Most solar finance companies in India provide extended loan repay tenures, making it easier for you to cover the EMIs and giving you ample time to recover the investment.
  • # Since Solar is a revenue generating asset and pays back the investment in a few years' time, it is prudent to choose financing for a solar plant if you are short of funds, since the additional money out-go can be easily recovered in only a few additional months of solar power generation.
